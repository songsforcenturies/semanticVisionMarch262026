## 1. GETTING STARTED

### 1.1 Accessing the Platform

Open your browser and navigate to the Semantic Vision platform URL. You will see the landing page with options to log in or create an account.

### 1.2 Unified Login System

Semantic Vision uses a single login page for all user types. At the top of the login form, you will see four icons representing different portal types:

| Icon | Portal | Authentication Method |
|---|---|---|
| Heart | Parents/Guardians | Email + Password |
| Graduation Cap | Students | Student Code + PIN |
| Book | Teachers | Email + Password |
| Megaphone | Brand Partners | Email + Password |

**To log in:**
1. Click the icon for your portal type
2. Enter your credentials
3. Click the login button
4. You will be redirected to your portal

### 1.3 Creating an Account

Click "Register Here" at the bottom of the login form. The registration page offers three account types:

| Role | Who Should Register | What You Need |
|---|---|---|
| Parents | Parents or guardians managing children's learning | Name, email, password |
| Teachers | Educators creating classroom sessions | Name, email, password |
| Brands | Companies sponsoring educational content | Company name, email, password |

**Note:** Student accounts are created BY parents through the Parent Portal. Students do not register themselves.

**Referral Codes:** If you received a referral code (REF-XXXXXX or AFF-XXXXXX), enter it during parent registration to receive rewards.

### 1.4 First-Login Onboarding

When you first log into any portal, a guided tutorial wizard will appear. This wizard walks you through the key features of your portal in 4-5 steps.

- **Click "Next"** to advance through steps
- **Click "Skip"** to dismiss the tutorial at any time
- **Click "Get Started"** on the last step to begin using the portal

**To replay the tutorial:** Click the "Tutorial" button in your portal's header area at any time.

### 1.5 Forgot Password

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page, enter your email, and check your inbox for a reset link.

## 2. PARENT/GUARDIAN GUIDE

The Parent Portal is your command center for managing your children's educational journey.

### 2.1 Dashboard Overview

After logging in, you'll see the following tabs:

| Tab | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Students** | Add and manage student profiles |
| **Subscription** | View and upgrade your plan |
| **Marketplace** | Browse and assign word banks |
| **Wallet** | Manage your digital wallet balance |
| **Invite & Earn** | Share referral codes for rewards |
| **Offers** | View promotional offers from brand partners |
| **Progress** | Track your children's reading and vocabulary growth |
| **FAQ** | Frequently asked questions |

### 2.2 Adding a Student

1. Navigate to the **Students** tab
2. Click **"Add Student"**
3. Follow the 5-step wizard:

**Step 1: Basic Information**
- Enter the student's full name
- Set their age
- Select their grade level (Pre-K through Adult)
- The grade level controls the vocabulary complexity of generated stories

**Step 2: Virtues & Life Lessons**
- Select from 16 character virtues (Courage, Honesty, Kindness, etc.)
- Each virtue includes a description
- Selected virtues will be woven into generated stories through character behavior

**Step 3: Strengths & Growth Areas**
- **Strengths:** Describe what your child is good at (e.g., "creative, good at math, kind to animals"). The AI will celebrate these as the story protagonist's "superpowers."
- **Growth Areas:** Describe areas for improvement (e.g., "sometimes impatient, struggles with reading aloud"). The AI will model growth in these areas through empathetic character development — never through shame.

**Step 4: Faith & Culture**
- **Belief System:** Select your family's faith tradition (Christianity, Islam, Buddhism, Hinduism, Judaism, Secular Humanism, etc.). Stories will reflect corresponding values.
- **Cultural Context:** Select your cultural background. Stories will incorporate culturally relevant names, settings, and traditions.

**Step 5: Word Banks**
- Assign vocabulary word banks to your student
- Word banks contain three tiers: Baseline (foundation), Target (growth), and Stretch (challenge)
- You can assign multiple word banks

4. Click **"Create Student"** to finish

After creation, you'll receive:
- A **Student Code** (e.g., STU-DR40V7) — your child uses this to log in
- A **PIN** (e.g., 914027) — a numeric password for the student

**Keep these credentials safe** — your child needs them to access their Academy.

### 2.3 Managing Students

From the Students tab, each student card shows:
- Student name, age, and grade level
- Student Code and PIN (click to reveal)
- Number of stories read
- Vocabulary words mastered
- Assigned word banks

Click a student card to edit their profile, interests, virtues, or assigned word banks.

### 2.4 Subscription Management

Navigate to the **Subscription** tab to:
- View your current plan (Free or Premium)
- See available plan options and pricing
- Apply coupon codes for discounts
- Upgrade or change your plan

**Free Plan:** Includes basic story generation with limited features.
**Premium Plans:** Unlock unlimited stories, advanced analytics, and premium features.

### 2.5 Marketplace

The **Marketplace** tab lets you:
- Browse available word banks (categorized as Included, Free, Paid, Specialized)
- View word bank contents (baseline, target, stretch words)
- Assign word banks to your students
- Create your own private word banks

**Creating a Private Word Bank:**
1. Click "Create Word Bank"
2. Enter a name and description
3. Add words to Baseline, Target, and Stretch tiers (with definitions and example sentences)
4. Your bank will be private — only visible to you and your students

### 2.6 Wallet

The **Wallet** tab provides:
- Current balance display (in your local currency)
- Transaction history
- Top-up functionality via Stripe (credit/debit card)
- Applied coupons and credits

### 2.7 Invite & Earn (Referral Program)

Share your referral code to earn rewards:
1. Your unique referral code is displayed on this tab
2. Share the code or link with friends
3. When they register using your code, you both earn rewards
4. Rewards may include: wallet credits, discounts, or flat fee payments (configured by admin)

### 2.8 Offers

The **Offers** tab shows promotional offers from brand partners:
- View offer titles, descriptions, and links
- Click through to offer destinations
- Dismiss individual offers you're not interested in
- **Toggle "Show Offers" off** to hide all brand offers entirely

### 2.9 Progress Tracking

The **Progress** tab shows each student's:
- **Vocabulary Mastered:** Count of unique words successfully learned
- **Stories Read:** Total stories completed
- **Agentic Reach Score:** A 0-100 score measuring how broadly reading is expanding their world (combines vocabulary mastery + reading engagement)
- **Word Banks:** Number of assigned word banks
- **Target:** Vocabulary target goal

### 2.10 Brand Content Preferences

By default, your children's stories do NOT include brand content. To enable or manage brand-integrated stories:
1. Edit a student's profile
2. In the Advertising Preferences section:
   - Toggle **"Allow Brand Stories"** on/off
   - Add categories to the **Blocked Categories** list to exclude specific types of brands

### 2.11 FAQ

The FAQ tab answers common parent questions about student management, story personalization, Agentic Reach Score, subscriptions, brand offers, and referrals.

## 3. STUDENT GUIDE

### 3.1 Logging In

1. Go to the login page
2. Click the **"Students"** icon (graduation cap)
3. Enter your **Student Code** (e.g., STU-DR40V7)
4. Enter your **PIN** (e.g., 914027)
5. Click **"Enter Academy"**

If you don't know your code or PIN, ask your parent or guardian.

### 3.2 Your Dashboard

After logging in, your Academy dashboard shows:
- **Stories Read:** How many stories you've completed
- **Vocabulary Mastered:** Words you've successfully learned
- **Agentic Reach Score:** Your overall reading growth score (0-100)
- Your recent stories

### 3.3 Reading a New Story

1. Click **"Create New Story"**
2. The AI will generate a personalized story just for you
3. Stories have **5 chapters**, each 300-500 words long
4. Highlighted words are your **vocabulary targets** — pay attention to them!

### 3.4 Comprehension Questions

After each chapter, you'll see a comprehension question with 4 answer choices:
1. Read the question carefully
2. Select the best answer
3. The system records whether you got it right
4. Don't worry about wrong answers — they help you learn!

### 3.5 Vocabulary Assessment

After finishing all 5 chapters, you'll take a vocabulary quiz:
1. For each vocabulary word, you'll be asked to:
   - **Define the word** in your own words
   - **Use it in a sentence**
2. The AI evaluates your answers for accuracy
3. Score 80% or higher to **master** those words
4. Mastered words are added to your permanent vocabulary count

### 3.6 Joining a Classroom Session

If your teacher starts a live reading session:
1. Ask your teacher for the **6-digit session code**
2. Enter the code in the "Join Classroom Session" area
3. You'll be connected to the live session
4. Follow along as the teacher controls the reading pace

### 3.7 Viewing Your Progress

Your dashboard always shows:
- Total **Vocabulary Mastered** count
- Total **Stories Read**
- Your **Agentic Reach Score**

### 3.8 Re-Reading Stories

All your stories are saved. Click any story card on your dashboard to read it again.

### 3.9 FAQ

Click the **"FAQ"** button in the header to see answers to common student questions about stories, vocabulary, classroom sessions, and scores.

## 5. BRAND PARTNER GUIDE

### 5.1 Creating a Brand Account

1. Go to the registration page
2. Click the **"Brands"** icon
3. Enter your company name, business email, and password
4. Click **Register**
5. Your account may require admin approval before full access

### 5.2 Your Dashboard

The Brand Portal includes these tabs:

| Tab | Purpose |
|---|---|
| **Dashboard** | Overview of brand performance |
| **Campaigns** | Create and manage ad campaigns |
| **Budget** | Monitor spending and top up budget |
| **Story Integrations** | See how your brand appears in stories |
| **Offers** | Create promotional offers for parents |
| **FAQ** | Frequently asked questions |
| **Analytics** | Detailed engagement metrics |

### 5.3 How Brand Story Integration Works

This is what makes Semantic Vision unique. Your brand is NOT shown as a traditional advertisement. Instead:

1. When a student requests a new story, the AI checks if your brand is eligible
2. If eligible, your brand's products are woven into the narrative as **organic, problem-solving elements**
3. For example: if your brand sells educational tablets, the story character might use your tablet to solve a school challenge
4. Students then answer **comprehension questions** — some of which may relate to how the brand product was used in the story
5. You receive analytics on ALL of this

### 5.4 Creating a Campaign

1. Go to the **Campaigns** tab
2. Click **"New Campaign"**
3. Fill in:
   - Campaign name
   - Products and descriptions (what your brand offers)
   - Problem statement (what problem your products solve)
   - Target age groups (leave empty for ALL age groups)
   - Target countries and languages
   - Daily budget and cost-per-impression rate
   - Brand logo URL
4. Click **Create**

### 5.5 Campaign Eligibility Rules

Your brand will appear in a student's story ONLY if ALL of these conditions are met:
- Your campaign is **active** (not paused)
- You have **remaining budget**
- The student's **guardian has opted in** to brand content
- Your brand's categories are **not on the guardian's blocked list**
- The student's **age falls within your target age range** (or you target all ages)
- The system-wide brand sponsorship feature is **enabled by admin**

### 5.6 Budget Management

The **Budget** tab shows:
- Total budget allocated
- Amount spent
- Remaining budget
- Cost per impression
- Top-up option to add more funds

Each time your brand appears in a story, a small amount (cost per impression, default $0.05) is deducted from your budget.

### 5.7 Story Integrations

The **Story Integrations** tab is your most powerful analytics tool:
- **Story Excerpts:** Actual sentences from student stories where your brand was mentioned
- **Brand Activation Questions:** Comprehension questions related to your brand
- **Student Responses:** How students answered those questions (pass/fail rates)
- **Full Story Access:** Read the complete story text

### 5.8 Creating Promotional Offers

Go to the **Offers** tab to create offers pushed directly to parents:
1. Click **"Create Offer"**
2. Enter title, description, and offer type (internal platform promo or external link)
3. Add the destination URL if external
4. Activate the offer

Parents will see your offer in their Offers tab. You can track views, clicks, and dismissals.

### 5.9 Analytics

The **Analytics** tab shows:
- Total impressions (how many stories featured your brand)
- Total stories featuring your brand
- Click-through rates
- Budget utilization
- Student engagement metrics

### 5.10 FAQ

The FAQ tab answers brand questions about story integration, campaign creation, offers, budgets, account approval, and demographic targeting.

### 6.3 Managing Users

The **Users** tab allows you to:

- View user details (role, registration date, plan, wallet balance)
- Edit user wallet balances (add or subtract credits)
- Change user roles
- Disable or delete accounts

### 6.4 Subscription Plans

The **Plans** tab lets you:
- View all subscription plans
- Create new plans with: name, price, story limits, features
- Edit existing plans
- Set plans as free or premium
- Define student seat limits per plan

### 6.5 Word Bank Management

The **Word Banks** tab is where you create and manage the vocabulary content that drives the platform:

1. Click **"Create Word Bank"**
2. Enter:
   - Bank name and description
   - Category (Included, Free, Paid, Specialized)
   - Specialty (if applicable)
3. Add words to three tiers:
   - **Baseline Words (60%):** Foundation vocabulary the student should already know
   - **Target Words (30%):** Growth vocabulary — the primary learning objective

Create time-bound referral competitions:
1. Set contest name, start/end dates
2. Define prizes (1st, 2nd, 3rd place)
3. Monitor the live leaderboard
4. Winners are determined by referral count

The **Brands** tab lets you:
- View all registered brand partners
- Approve or reject new brand applications
- View brand details: budget, impressions, products, revenue generated
- Toggle brand active/inactive status
- Create new brand sponsor profiles

### 6.8 Affiliate Program Management

The **Affiliates** tab provides full control over the referral program:

**Program Settings:**
- **Reward Type:** Choose between Flat Fee, Percentage, or Wallet Credits
- **Flat Fee Amount:** Dollar amount per referral (e.g., $7.50)
- **Percentage Rate:** Percentage of first purchase (e.g., 15%)

- **Auto-Approve:** Whether new affiliates are automatically approved

**Affiliate Management:**
- View all affiliates with: code, referral count, earned amount, pending payout
- Approve pending affiliates
- Deactivate problematic affiliates
- Record payouts (marks pending balance as paid)

### 6.9 System Configuration

As admin, you control:
- **LLM Model Selection:** Which AI model generates stories
- **Brand Sponsorship Feature Flag:** Enable/disable brand content platform-wide
- **Default Cost Per Impression:** How much brands pay per story inclusion
- **Platform-wide settings** affecting all users

## 7. GLOSSARY

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **Agentic Reach Score** | A proprietary metric (0-100) measuring a student's vocabulary acquisition rate relative to their reading engagement. Formula: min((mastered_tokens × 10 + completed_narratives × 50) / max(total_narratives × 50, 1) × 100, 100.0) |
| **Baseline Words** | Foundation-level vocabulary (60% of story content) that reinforces existing knowledge |
| **Target Words** | Growth-level vocabulary (30%) representing the primary learning objective |
| **Stretch Words** | Challenge-level vocabulary (10%) introducing aspirational language |
| **Brand Activation Question** | A comprehension question specifically related to how a brand product was used in the story narrative |
| **Brand Impression** | A single instance of a brand product appearing in a generated student story |
| **Vision Check** | A per-chapter multiple-choice comprehension question |
| **Word Bank** | A curated collection of vocabulary words organized into baseline, target, and stretch tiers |
| **Student Code** | A unique identifier (e.g., STU-DR40V7) used by students to log in |
| **Affiliate Code** | A unique referral code (e.g., AFF-XXXXXX) used to track referral signups |
| **Narrative** | An AI-generated, multi-chapter educational story personalized for a specific student |
| **Cost Per Impression (CPI)** | The amount debited from a brand's budget each time their product appears in a story |
| **Consent Gate** | The default-false opt-in mechanism requiring guardian approval for brand content |
| **60/30/10 Model** | The proprietary tiered vocabulary distribution system used in story generation |
